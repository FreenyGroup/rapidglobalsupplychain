Company Overview

Rapid for Supply Chain and Shipping Consultancy and Services, was established in 1983 under Kogali brothers’ group – Currently runs as Rapid for Supply Chain and Shipping Consultancy and Services, it has five branches:

United States, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, and Sudan (Khartoum and Port Sudan).

We have a network all-over the globe with more than 100 third-party Logistics companies and freight forwarder partners, thus we are available 24/7 for inquiries, RFQs.

Vision

To become number one name in Sudan and MENA re- gion by providing the most comprehensive, reliable, and cost-effective Supply Chain management solu- tions to our customers while maintaining the highest level of customer satisfaction.

Mission

Our mission is to provide superior, innovative, and top-quality logistics services that improve business solution for our customers. Our goal is to add value for clients, business partners and principals through restless pursuit of excellence and unswerving focus on fulfilling customer needs.
