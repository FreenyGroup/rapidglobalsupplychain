Company Overview

Rapid for Supply Chain and Shipping Consultancy and Services, was established in 1983 under Kogali brothers’ group – Currently runs as Rapid for Supply Chain and Shipping Consultancy and Services, it has five branches:

United States, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, and Sudan (Khartoum and Port Sudan).

We have a network all-over the globe with more than 100 third-party Logistics companies and freight forwarder partners, thus we are available 24/7 for inquiries, RFQs.

What We Do

End-to-End Supply Chain Services

End to end (E2E) supply chain involves an entire integrated pro- cess. From product design and procurement of raw materials then scheduling, production and then final delivery of finished product to the customer. It is further extended to after-sales service and reverse logistics depending on the nature of the business.

Sourcing/Procurement

Rapid provides clients with innovative Sourcing and Procurement Services. Through these supply chain services, we help companies to achieve both monetary savings and improved supplier relation- ships. We offer a variety of value-added services, including Cate- gory Management, Contingency Based Cost Reduction, Contract- ing, Negotiations, Strategic Sourcing Services, and more.

Shipping & Freight Forwarding

  • Air, Ocean and Land Freight Forwarding
  • All export, import, cross stuffing and cross trade shipments.
  • International and local land transport logistics solutions.
  • FCL & LCL consignment around the world.
  • Container transportation, Land transportation, LTL & FTL.

Transport

Our transportation services offer unmatched land transport solu- tions with a range of vehicles that have the capacity you need to move your cargo

Relocation

Rapid guarantees a hassle-free domestic relocation service across Saudi Arabia & Sudan and the rest of the countries because we pride ourselves on taking customer service to the next level. We take care of dismantling and removal of fitting/furniture and pack- ing of all your belongings while making sure they are safe and in- sured before transport. We work with only licensed and experi- enced moving crew to help you carefully relocate your household or business to your desired location.

Warehousing

  • Strategically located warehouses across multiple locations around the world.
  • Easy access to all major sea, air & road networks.
  • Space available at both free zone & local warehouses.
  • Equipment available on-site for off-loading and on-loading cargo.
  • Temperature controlled warehouse, Labeling, Pick & pack.

Cargo Handling & Packaging

  • We are a licensed clearance agency.
  • Authorized for cargo clearance and forwarding globally.
  • Experienced ground staff for cargo handling process
  • Experienced professionals for unpacking & repacking
  • Serving in all the major countries.

Customs Clearance

  • Custom clearing at all commercial ports of entry in the UAE and Sudan and all Countries.
  • All export/import & transit clearances by Sea, Air and Land.
  • Immediate processing of custom clearing with all documentation.
  • Dedicated staff for coordination and updates
  • Best custom clearing rates guarantee.

Personal Effects Shipping

Whether you’re moving overseas or just shipping a few household items, you want peace of mind that your international shipping com- pany will treat your valuable personal belongings with the utmost care. With door-to-door and port-to-port service, our experienced staff will provide comprehensive estimates for all your personal effects .

Stevedoring services

Loading and unloading ships is specialized work. With a century worth of experience, we can say we are that specialist. Our per- sonnel is expert and passionate; our equipment is state of the art. We focus on delivering quality, so that we are able to load and un- load your products with great care and speed. We can even make up for time lost in preceding links of the chain. ‘Delay recovery’ means we try to close up the logistical chain so goods are deliv- ered on time. We handle a broad range of cargo, from break bulk to high and heavy and from project cargo to containers

Chartering service (Aircraft and vessel chartering)

When your deadline or unique project cargo demands a dedicated vessel or aircraft, Rapid can coordinate global access to charter capacity. We help you translate complex project requirements into transportable solutions, making sure your operational and finan- cial targets are met.
